Outputs

What Ceezaer Generates from Your Data

🗺️

Orthomosaic Maps

Geo-accurate 2D maps of your entire site — measurable, overlayable with plans, and shareable with your team.

🏔️

3D Point Clouds

Full 3D representations of site conditions for volume calculations, clash detection, and as-built documentation.

📐

Surface Models

Digital elevation and terrain models for cut/fill analysis, drainage planning, and grading verification.

🔗

BIM Overlay

Compare your drone-captured as-built data against your BIM model to identify deviations automatically.

⏩

Progress Timeline

A complete visual history of your project — swipe between any two dates to see exactly what changed.

📦

Volume Calculations

AI-calculated stockpile volumes, cut/fill quantities, and material movement tracking from aerial data.
