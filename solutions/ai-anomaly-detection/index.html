What AI Flags

Every Anomaly Type We Detect

📐

Structural Deviations from Plans

AI compares as-built aerial data against design plans, flagging walls, columns, and structural elements out of tolerance.

⛑️

Safety Hazards on Site

Detects unsecured materials, missing fall protection, open excavations, and unsafe equipment staging.

💧

Water Intrusion & Drainage

Identifies ponding water, drainage failures, and moisture infiltration that cause long-term structural damage.

📦

Material Stockpile Changes

Tracks material delivery, usage, and stockpile depletion — alerting to unexpected changes that signal theft or waste.

🚧

Unauthorized Access

Detects unauthorized structures, encroachments, or activity in restricted site areas.

🔥

Thermal Anomalies

Infrared analysis detects electrical hot spots, insulation gaps, and moisture-related thermal signatures.
