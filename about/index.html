About

The Story Behind the Lens

Jerry L. Brooks

Aviation Roots, Cinematic Vision

With 20+ years of aviation experience, I'm Jerry L. Brooks—known professionally as Ceezaer—a professional drone cinematographer and creative media specialist based in Austin, Texas. My passion is capturing the world from extraordinary perspectives that transform how people see their projects, properties, and stories.

My work spans multiple industries: from real estate and commercial advertising to documentary filmmaking and creative content production. I bring technical precision from aviation together with an artistic eye for composition, light, and movement. Aerial cinematography isn't just about flying a drone—it's about telling stories from angles that were once impossible to achieve.

Every flight is carefully planned with attention to composition, lighting, safety, and narrative purpose. I use professional-grade equipment and stay current with the latest techniques to deliver footage that elevates projects above the ordinary.

Approach & Philosophy

I believe great aerial work requires more than technical skill—it demands understanding what the footage needs to accomplish. Every project begins with listening: understanding your goals, your audience, and what success looks like for your specific situation.

Whether documenting a property for sale, capturing footage for a film, or inspecting infrastructure, I approach each flight with the same commitment to safety, quality, and delivering results that exceed expectations.

I work with clients across Central Texas who value professionalism, clear communication, and work that speaks for itself. If that resonates with you, I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss your project.
