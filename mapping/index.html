Who We Are

Ceezaer provides geospatial and aerial data services using professional drone mapping workflows. Based in Austin, TX, we deliver accurate, actionable mapping products for commercial projects across Central Texas. Every flight is planned around safety and regulatory compliance, and every deliverable is built for clarity—so stakeholders can make informed decisions without sifting through raw data. Our focus is straightforward: capture the site, process the data, and deliver results you can act on.

Why Drone Mapping Saves Money

Practical Advantages

  • Faster site documentation vs. manual methods
  • Fewer site visits and reduced labor costs
  • Better progress tracking and reporting
  • Improved planning and communication across stakeholders
  • Early detection of issues (grade, stockpiles, layout)
  • Verifiable records for dispute resolution and compliance
