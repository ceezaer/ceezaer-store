Insurance Coverage

Commercial General Liability

Ceezaer carries $1,500,000 in commercial general liability coverage for qualifying drone projects. Professional insurance is a cornerstone of responsible commercial drone operations, providing peace of mind for clients, property owners, and all stakeholders involved in aerial production work.

This coverage ensures that every commercial engagement is backed by comprehensive liability protection, meeting or exceeding the requirements of most venues, property managers, and production companies.

Coverage Details

What's Included

  • $1,500,000 Commercial General Liability -- Full coverage for bodily injury and property damage arising from commercial drone operations, protecting clients and third parties.
  • Certificate of Insurance (COI) Available -- A COI can be provided upon request for qualifying projects, naming your organization as an additional insured as needed.
  • Compliant with Industry Standards -- Coverage meets the insurance requirements of major venues, production companies, real estate firms, and government agencies across Texas.
