FAQ

Common Questions

Is there really no credit card required for the trial?
Correct. Start the 14-day trial with just your email. We'll ask for payment details at the end of the trial period only if you choose to continue.
What drone brands and file formats do you support?
We support JPEG, PNG, TIFF images and MP4/MOV video from any drone manufacturer including DJI, Autel, Skydio, and others. Geotagged images are processed with higher accuracy.
Can I use the platform if Ceezaer captures the footage for me?
Yes — this is the most common workflow. Ceezaer flies your site on a schedule, uploads the data, and you receive reports automatically. Platform access is included with all construction monitoring service contracts.
How secure is my aerial data?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use role-based access control, and enterprise plans include dedicated data isolation — your data is never shared or used to train models without explicit consent.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard or by emailing info@ceezaer.com. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Yes — annual billing saves 20% on both Starter and Growth plans. Contact us to set up annual billing or switch from monthly.
