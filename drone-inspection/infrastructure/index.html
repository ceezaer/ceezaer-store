FAQ

Common Questions

Is drone inspection accepted for bridge compliance reporting?
Drone inspection data is increasingly accepted as supplemental documentation for FHWA bridge inspections. Reports are formatted to meet standard inspection protocols. Always confirm with your agency's specific requirements.
How does thermal imaging help infrastructure inspection?
Thermal cameras detect subsurface moisture, electrical hot spots, and delamination that are invisible to standard cameras. This is especially valuable for bridge deck delamination and substation equipment inspection.
Can drones access confined or hard-to-reach structures?
Drones can access locations that are dangerous or impractical for human inspectors — undersides of bridges, interior tower faces, and tall structures — safely and without scaffolding.
