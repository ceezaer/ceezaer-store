Our Services

Drone Services Available in Round Rock & Austin Metro

🏠

Real Estate Photography

Aerial stills and video for residential and commercial listings. We cover Round Rock, Georgetown, Cedar Park, Leander, Pflugerville, and all surrounding suburbs. Same-week scheduling, 24-hour edited delivery, MLS-ready files. Starting at $175.

🔍

Roof Inspections

Residential and commercial roof inspections using RGB and thermal imaging. Ideal for post-hail damage assessment, pre-sale inspections, and insurance claims. We serve property owners, roofing contractors, and insurance adjusters across Round Rock and Williamson County.

🏗️

Construction Monitoring

Weekly or bi-weekly aerial progress documentation for residential subdivisions, commercial developments, and infrastructure projects. Austin metro has over $2B in active construction — Ceezaer tracks progress for GCs and developers from Georgetown to Kyle.

🗺️

Drone Mapping & Surveying

Orthomosaic maps, 3D models, and volumetric measurements for land development, earthwork, and site planning. Deliverables compatible with AutoCAD, Civil 3D, and GIS platforms. Serving Williamson County, Travis County, and surrounding areas.

🎬

Commercial Aerial Cinematography

Corporate video, event coverage, promotional content, and documentary-style aerial cinematography. We've shot for Austin-area businesses, developers, and media companies. Cinematic 4K footage with professional color grading.

⚡

Solar & Infrastructure Inspection

Thermal inspection of solar panel arrays to identify underperforming panels, hotspots, and wiring issues. Also available for cell towers, water towers, and commercial structures. Faster and safer than traditional inspection methods.
