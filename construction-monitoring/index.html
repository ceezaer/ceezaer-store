FAQ

Common Questions

How often should I capture for construction monitoring?
Most projects benefit from weekly aerial captures. High-pace phases like structural steel erection may use bi-weekly or daily captures during critical milestones.
What construction phases can drone monitoring track?
All phases: site prep and grading, foundation, structural framing, envelope and roofing, MEP rough-in, and interior finishing through punch list completion.
Can I share reports directly with my owner or lender?
Yes. Reports are generated as branded PDFs and shareable dashboard links — formatted specifically for owner and lender progress reviews.
Does aerial monitoring replace site walks?
It complements them. Aerial monitoring provides a birds-eye perspective, historical comparison, and automatic documentation that ground walks cannot replace.
What areas does Ceezaer serve for on-site capture?
We capture within 48km of Round Rock, TX covering Austin, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, and Leander. The analytics platform is available nationwide for self-upload.
