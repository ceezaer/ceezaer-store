Inspections & Surveys

Austin, Round Rock & Central Texas

Safer, Faster, More Detailed Inspections

Traditional inspections put people at risk and miss critical details. Drone inspections eliminate the need for scaffolding, lifts, or climbing—delivering comprehensive visual documentation in a fraction of the time and cost.

As an FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot with professional-grade equipment, I provide detailed aerial inspections for roofs, construction sites, cell towers, solar installations, and infrastructure throughout Austin, Round Rock, and Central Texas.

Every inspection includes high-resolution imagery, annotated reports identifying areas of concern, and recommendations for follow-up action. Documentation suitable for insurance claims, maintenance planning, and compliance reporting.

Why Drone Inspections?

The Advantages

  • Safety — No need for personnel to climb, use lifts, or work at heights
  • Speed — Complete inspections in hours instead of days
  • Access — Reach areas impossible or dangerous for human inspectors
  • Detail — High-resolution imagery captures details invisible from ground level
  • Cost — Lower total cost than traditional inspection methods
  • Documentation — Permanent visual record for insurance, compliance, and planning
