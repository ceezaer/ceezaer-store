Drone Services

Mapping & Surveying

Austin, Round Rock & Central Texas

Precision Data From Above

Drone mapping transforms how professionals visualize, measure, and document land. Whether you're planning a construction project, managing agricultural operations, or documenting property boundaries, aerial mapping delivers accuracy and efficiency that ground surveys cannot match.

As an FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot, I provide comprehensive mapping services throughout Austin, Round Rock, and Central Texas. Using professional-grade equipment and specialized software, I create orthomosaic maps, 3D models, topographic data, and volumetric calculations that support informed decision-making.

Every mapping project is executed with precision flight planning, overlapping imagery for maximum accuracy, and professional post-processing to deliver actionable data you can trust.

Mapping Services

Data Products Available

🗺️

Orthomosaic Maps

High-resolution, geometrically corrected aerial maps. Measure distances, calculate areas, and analyze terrain with survey-grade accuracy.

🏔️

3D Models

Detailed three-dimensional models of sites, structures, and terrain. Visualize projects, plan modifications, and share with stakeholders.

📊

Elevation Data

Digital Surface Models (DSM) and Digital Terrain Models (DTM) for topographic analysis, drainage planning, and grading design.

📐

Volumetric Analysis

Calculate stockpile volumes, cut/fill quantities, and material estimates with accuracy that improves project planning and cost control.

⏰

Progress Tracking

Regular mapping flights to document construction progress, compare to plans, and create time-lapse visualizations of project development.

🌾

Agricultural Analysis

Crop health assessment, irrigation planning, and field analysis using NDVI and multispectral imaging where applicable.

Why Drone Mapping?

The Advantages

  • Speed — Survey large areas in hours instead of days or weeks
  • Cost — Significantly lower cost than traditional survey methods for many applications
  • Frequency — Affordable to repeat surveys for progress tracking and change detection
  • Safety — No personnel required on potentially hazardous terrain
  • Visualization — Rich visual data that communicates clearly to all stakeholders
  • Integration — Data exports to CAD, GIS, and project management software
