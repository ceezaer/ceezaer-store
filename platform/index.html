FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Ceezaer platform work?
Upload drone footage or images to Ceezaer. Our AI analyzes the aerial data to detect construction progress, identify anomalies, compare against previous captures, and generate automated professional reports for your stakeholders.
Do I need my own drone to use the platform?
No. You can upload footage from any drone — or hire Ceezaer's FAA Part 107 certified pilots to capture aerial data for you. We also partner with drone service providers nationwide.
What file formats does Ceezaer accept?
Ceezaer accepts JPEG, PNG, TIFF images and MP4/MOV video from any drone manufacturer including DJI, Autel, and Skydio.
How quickly are reports generated?
Most reports are generated within minutes of upload. Large datasets or 3D model processing may take up to an hour.
Is my aerial data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use role-based access control and retain data for audit compliance. Enterprise plans include dedicated data isolation.
