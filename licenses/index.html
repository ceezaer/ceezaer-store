Licenses

Professional Certifications & Credentials

Operating drones commercially requires proper certification and compliance with federal regulations. Ceezaer maintains all necessary licenses and credentials to conduct professional drone operations legally and safely. Below are the current certifications held for aerial cinematography and commercial drone services.

📜

FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate

Federally certified by the Federal Aviation Administration for commercial drone operations. This certification authorizes Ceezaer to operate unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) for commercial purposes in the National Airspace System, in compliance with 14 CFR Part 107.

📡

FCC Radio License

Licensed by the Federal Communications Commission as an authorized radio frequency operator. This license ensures compliance with FCC regulations for the operation of radio transmission equipment used in professional drone and communication systems.

If you have questions about licensing, certifications, or regulatory compliance for your drone project, don't hesitate to reach out.
